The aim of the game is to find all 8 matching geckos.

To play the game, click the new game button and turn over an initial card and click again to try to find a matching pair.

If you find a match, both cards will remain turned over and no longer be clickable.

If you don't find a match, the cards will be hidden again and you can try again.

The game is finished when all the pairs have been found.
